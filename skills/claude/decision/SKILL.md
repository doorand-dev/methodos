# /decision

Clarify the observable outcome, compare viable options and maintenance cost, and
choose the smallest testable path. Ask the user before external work, user data,
permissions, database/schema changes, public interfaces, concurrency/migrations,
or other external state.

Create an ADR only when the decision is hard to reverse, surprising without
context, or a genuine trade-off a future maintainer needs. Otherwise close in
the answer without a WHY comment, report, hash, or attestation.
