# Grill Me

Search the existing caller/producer/consumer first, then ask one plain-language
question at a time until the user-visible goal, scope, affected flow, and
acceptance checks are clear. New multi-file or multi-flow work may receive one
optional fresh-context `spec-novelist` narrative; routine fixes do not.

Persist a spec only when a future maintainer needs it. Obtain explicit user
approval before changing a public flow or contract, permissions/data,
irreversible operation, or external state. Otherwise answer with the resolved
decision and proceed.

This skill does not require reports, hashes, lineage, terminal packets,
attestation, or an automatic verify loop.
